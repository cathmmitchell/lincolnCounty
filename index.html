Fun things to do in Lincoln County

Created by Cath

Hiking

Visit one of the Damariscotta River Association or the Pemaquid Watershed Association preserves or the Boothbay Region Land Trust.

Siteseeing

Visit Pemaquid Lighthouse and Museum.

On the water

Why not take a boat ride? Check out the Hardy Boat from New Harbor to see some puffins, go whale watching or visit Monhegan Island. There are options from Boothbay Harbor too.
